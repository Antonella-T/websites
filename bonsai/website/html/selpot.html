Selecting a Pot:

Selecting the proper pot of your bonsai is as important as selecting the style that brings out the natural beauty of the tree. There are many types of pots, ranging from simple clay to glazed ceramic pots with hand painted detailing. Some pots are designed for indoor use while others are designed for outdoor use.
