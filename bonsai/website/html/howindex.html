Selecting a Plant:

Almost any plant can be crafted into a beautiful and balanced bonsai. After all, bonsai simply translated means “plant in pot”; it doesn’t say what kind of plant.

Selecting a Style:

There are five basic styles of bonsai: formal upright, informal upright, slanting, cascade, and semi-cascade. The style you choose for your tree will depend largely on the natural characteristics of the tree.

Selecting a Pot:

Selecting the proper pot of your bonsai is as important as selecting the style that brings out the natural beauty of the tree.

Maintenance:

Creating the bonsai is a first and important part of the bonsai art; caring for your bonsai is a second and no less important part. Indoor plant specimens will require different care than outdoor specimens. With the right care and maintenance, your bonsai will bring you hears of happiness and tranquility.
